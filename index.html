About...

I remember the time that I was taking art classes. Some people would paint first but, I had to do sketch first to paint on paper. When I do sketching, there is one thing that I can never forget to bring. An eraser. Otherwise, my painting would've looked really off. It worth every moment. Taking art classes made me realize that it is a very important to revise early on to make things look good to bring an idea into reality.

Through painting, I also realized that I have an incredible talent that I've overlooked... which is creative artistic expressions that I can bring to life as well as problem solving skills. Now days, I love utilizing the skills that I have with the digital world tools such as HTML and CSS and jQuery. I love working in a digital world and I can't wait to be part of a team to work together with people to bring more people from all over the world to enjoy staying online for everyday life.
